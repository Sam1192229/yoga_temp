YOUR GUIDE TO DO IT RIGHT

Posture in yoga refers to the way the body is held while standing, sitting, or lying down. Good posture is essential for overall health and wellbeing, as it helps to prevent pain, injury, and fatigue. In yoga, proper posture is important for maintaining balance and stability in each asana.

Poor posture can cause muscle imbalances, leading to pain and discomfort. For example, slouching in a seated position can cause strain on the neck and back muscles, leading to stiffness and pain. Proper alignment and posture are essential for a safe and effective yoga practice. They help to prevent injuries, improve flexibility, and enhance the benefits of each asana. In addition, good alignment and posture help to develop body awareness, which is essential for developing a deeper understanding of the practice.
