Posture Detection

Posture in yoga refers to the way the body is held while standing, sitting, or lying down. Good posture is essential for overall health and wellbeing, as it helps to prevent pain, injury, and fatigue. In yoga, proper posture is important for maintaining balance and stability in each asana.
